Work and Time Calculations

Problem Solution
Days required for A, B, and C to complete work Calculated based on cyclic work done by A, B, and C.
Extra workers required to finish work in 8 days 42 extra workers are required.
Work completion with additional workers Calculated based on work done by additional workers.
Days required to build a wall Calculated based on man-hours and volume of the wall.
Time for Pipe B to fill the tank alone Calculated based on the rate of both pipes.
Work done by P and Q together Calculated based on their work rates.
