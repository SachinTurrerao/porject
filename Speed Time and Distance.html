Math Problems and Solutions

1. Meeting Point of Two People Moving Between X and Y

Two places X and Y are 200 meters apart. P moves at 20 km/h and Q moves at 30 km/h. When they meet for the first time, the time taken can be calculated using relative speed. Relative speed = (20 + 30) km/h = 50 km/h. Time = Distance / Speed = 200 meters / (50 km/h converted to m/s) = 14.4 seconds approximately.
