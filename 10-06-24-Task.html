Prabhas - Wikipedia

Prabhas

Summary

Prabhas is an Indian actor known for his work in Telugu, Tamil, and Hindi films. He gained international fame with his role in the Baahubali series.

Details

Prabhas was born on October 23, 1979, in Chennai, India. He made his acting debut with the movie Eeshwar in 2002. Since then, he has starred in numerous successful films, including Mirchi, Billa, and Saaho.

Prabhas is known for his dedication to his roles, often undergoing extensive physical transformations. His portrayal of Amarendra Baahubali and Mahendra Baahubali in the Baahubali series earned him critical acclaim.

Outside of acting, Prabhas is passionate about fitness and often performs his own stunts. He is also involved in philanthropic activities, supporting various causes.
